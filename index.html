Hey, I'm Ilan.

I'm a sophomore at Belmont Hill School, and I spend most of my free time tinkering with robots, writing code, and figuring out how to make things work. I got into tech because I was always curious about what makes stuff tick—that curiosity eventually turned into building actual projects.

Right now I'm working on a prosthetic hand that uses flex sensors to mimic finger movements. It's still a work in progress (lots of trial and error), but I'm learning a ton along the way. I'm also part of our school's drone team, which has been pretty cool—we're currently ranked first nationally, though honestly that's more of a team effort than anything I did alone.
